ABOUT US

Online Tuckshop is a joint venture runned by students of computer science in Lovely Professional University. It is one stop online and depot store for a huge variety of stationary products such as copies, pages , registers etc.We also printing,scanning and spiral binding for our customers. Our aim is to provide a quality products and fulfill customer needs with prime focus on reasonable prices and availablity of product in time.

Great value prices and really available stock is our main stregth and we use it well for attracting our potential customers.
